Edureka Overview

About Edureka

Edureka is an online learning platform founded in 2011. It offers a wide range of courses in various fields, including technology, data science, and business management. The platform gained popularity for its interactive learning approach, which includes live instructor-led classes, hands-on projects, and a strong focus on practical skills. Edureka has expanded its offerings over the years and is known for its emphasis on helping professionals advance their careers through skill development.
